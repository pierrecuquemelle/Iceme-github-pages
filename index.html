Questions?

Q: I don't get it! What can Lokal do for me?

Lokal is a platform which enables tourists to interact with selected independent local guides through a chat service. Each guide is specialised in a specific area, providing useful recommendations and tips, and negotiating the best prices. You don't have to bargain prices and go through the time-consuming process of comparing offers anymore!

Q: What are the differences with a travel agency?

Local travel agencies have to maintain high prices to cover their costs while online travel agencies don’t have the local knowledge to offer you the best. Our local guides operate online, so that they can provide you with the most interesting transportation options, tours and activities at the lowest prices.
Moreover, while most agencies only provide listings of activities that you have to choose from, Lokal revolutionises this model and focuses on the user-experience through one-to-one communication. Lokal guides take the time to understand your preferences, in order to give you the best advice and ensure a memorable experience. They also help you with all questions you might have during your stay, such as getting a local sim-card or booking a bus/train to your next destination.

Q: How much does it cost?

It's completely free to download and use the Lokal app. Our guides are in touch with service providers to negotiate discounts and offer you activities (tours, sports, etc.) and transportation options (scooter and car rentals, drivers, boats, buses, etc.) at the lowest prices.

Q: How do you guarantee the quality of the services you offer?

Lokal guides curate a selection of the top activities for your holidays. They use their local knowledge, but also read travel guides, search the web and study reviews to make sure you always get the best. All the activities offered by Lokal guides have been tested and went through an extensive review process.

Q: How quickly will I get an answer?

Lokal guides are available to chat 24/7 for questions related to their respective areas.

Where is Lokal available?

Lokal is currently available in Bali - Indonesia. Selected areas in Vietnam, Thailand and Cambodia will be added in the next weeks, before global expansion.
